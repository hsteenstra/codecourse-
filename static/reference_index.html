CodeCourse App

A new way for teachers and students to learn, teach, and grow together.

CodeCourse is our new app built for teachers and students. It makes learning multiple coding languages simple, structured, and exciting — all in one place.

Teachers can create a classroom, add students, assign lessons, and track progress. Students can learn at their own pace with guided lessons, quizzes, and videos — all created by me.

This is a core part of Code For ME, and it’s designed to make coding accessible for every classroom.

About Code For ME

Hi! I'm Harper, the founder of Code For ME- a local coding camp designed for beginner coders. I believe coding is essential to the future. Whether you're building a toaster or designing the next iPhone, code is behind it all.

I first discovered coding back in third grade, dragging colorful blocks across the screen. I instantly fell in love. But once that class ended, I realized how few programming opportunities were available for students like me.

After years of learning computer science through online and in-person courses, I'm now ready to help your child start their coding journey. Thank you for being here — and welcome to Code For ME!

Interested In Signing Up?

Thank you so much for your interest in Code For ME! We're thrilled to see your excitement about learning and exploring the world of coding.

At this time, we do not have any upcoming camps or programs scheduled. While we're currently on pause, we're working behind the scenes to plan meaningful and engaging experiences for future participants. Whether you're a student, parent, or educator, we truly appreciate your support and enthusiasm.

We encourage you to check back here regularly for updates on future camp opportunities. You can also follow us on social media or join our mailing list to be among the first to know when new events are announced.

In the meantime, feel free to explore our site to learn about our past programs. We invite you to explore our Code At Home section-- a great way to keep learning and building your skills from anywhere. We've gathered a free range of high-quality resources, activities, and vocab that you can use at your own pace. Whether you're just looking to get started or to take your skills to the next level, there's something for everyone.

We'll See You Next Time,
